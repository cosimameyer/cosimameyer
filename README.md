# Hi, I'm Cosima 👋

**Data Scientist | Open Source Developer | Speaker | Women Techmakers Ambassador**

I bridge the gap between social science research and production-grade data science. I’m passionate about building tools that make data more accessible and sharing that knowledge through international talks and workshops.

[🌐 Website](https://cosimameyer.com) • [💼 LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cosimameyer/) • [🐘 Mastodon](https://mas.to/@cosima_meyer) • [👩🏼‍🏫 Speaker Profile](https://speakerinnen.org/de/profiles/6467)

### 🎤 Speaking & Workshops
I love speaking at the intersection of technology and society. My recent sessions focus on:
* **Explainable AI & ML:** Building ML products and breaking down complex models (NLP/LLMs) into interpretable, actionable insights.
* **The Bridge:** Navigating the transition between Social Science research and production Data Science.
* **Community Building:** Fostering inclusive tech spaces.
